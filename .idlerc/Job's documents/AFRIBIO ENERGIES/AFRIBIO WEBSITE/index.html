The Firewood Trap: A Triple Threat to Education

Financial Drain

Schools spend Ksh 70,000-100,000+ monthly on firewood, totaling Ksh 630,000-900,000+ annually - funds that should be invested in education.

Health & Safety Crisis

Kitchen staff face respiratory illnesses from smoke exposure and safety risks during firewood collection. Students' health is also compromised.

Operational Instability

Unreliable supply chains and price volatility make firewood an unpredictable energy source, disrupting school meal preparation.

Our Solution: Modern Biogas Systems

We provide fully automated, self-sustaining bio-energy systems that transform a school's organic waste into clean, free cooking gas.

  • Eliminates fuel costs completely
  • Ends dangerous firewood collection
  • Creates a cleaner, healthier kitchen environment
  • Guarantees reliable energy supply
  • Solves waste management challenges
